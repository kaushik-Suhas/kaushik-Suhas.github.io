Academic Interests

Six Sigma

Six Sigma is a set of methods and tools for process improvement. Developed in 1986, Six Sigma has become a global phenomenon with companies around the world. Companies and individuals are using six sigma methodologies to improve operational efficiencies. Six Sigma emphasizes more on eliminating errors within processes. Major benefits of Six Sigma strategy includes customer satisfaction and cost reduction. It leads to revenue growth and productivity. Lean Six Sigma is a method which provides prized analytic and process skills. My Six Sigma Green Belt cerification was done through Linköping University in December 2018.

Quality & Operations Management

​Continuous improvement of business processes is of increasing importance for any organizations competing in the global market. For society, it is very important to sustain work opportunities by realising and utilising the potential in existing businesses. Companies need to compete in both ways by creating new products and by improving existing products and processes to the market. This is where Quality & Operations management comes into picture and is one among my interests which I would love to pursue.

Supply Chain Management

Supply Chain Management is considered as an important aspect in any organization and it deals with the management of flow of goods, services and all the process that involves in transforming the raw material into finished product. It involves the activities that maximize the customer value and gain a competitive advantage in the marketplace.

Lean Production

The word lean production suggests the production of any product with minimum waste. Waste Minimization is arguably the most significant benefit to any system. Lean production can efficiently minimize wastage of resources within a production unit considerably. As most of the companies have large inventories and thus waste, this process eliminates aged or outdated inventories.

Logistics

Logistics management is the control and supervision of the movement of goods. For any business organization to be successful Logistics plays an important role. Logistics Reduce Expenses for Effective Cost Accounting. Logistics is considered as one of the important tool in creating values for the customer.
